Who we are

We are Rooftop Noodles, a Vietnamese restaurant looking to do a more modern take on Vietnamese cuisine while also providing an experience that ties down to its roots. Located in the middle of a bustling city, Rooftop Noodles has a rooftop bar where we serve our cuisine along with Asian-inspired beverages for all ages to create an unforgettable experience.
